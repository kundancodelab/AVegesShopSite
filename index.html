About Champaran Sabji Bhandar

Champaran Sabji Bhandar is a trusted vegetable shop located in Bazar Samiti near Hariwatika Shiv Mandir in Bettiah, West Champaran. Fresh vegetables are selected daily from the local mandi to ensure quality and fair prices for customers.

The shop is managed by Ravi Kumar Gupta, who focuses on providing fresh and quality vegetables to customers every day.
